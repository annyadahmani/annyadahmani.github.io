Research

Children's ability to learn so much about the physical and social world so rapidly and flexibly is ubiquitous. My research focuses on leveraging experimental and computational approaches to better understand the link between learning and exploration. How can we build artificial intelligence that draws directly from human cognition? Why is the development of Theory of Mind so crucial to our understanding of what makes humans social beings? How can we build mentalizing machines?

Reserach interests: cognitive development, computational cognitive science, social cognition, explortation, learning, artificial intelligence.
